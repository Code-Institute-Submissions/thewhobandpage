About the Band

What style do we play?

We have played rock music with many cult-favorites known today.

How long have we made music?

The original band started playing together back in 1964. Two of the original four members have died over the years.

Why listen to us?

We have toured now for 54 years all around the world and still do so.. There are only three bands who have more experience, The Rolling Stones, The Beach boys, and Golden Earring.

Biography

The Who was formed in 1962 and still has the remainder of the band preforming gigs around the world. With over 100 million records they are in many lists for the top 100 best bands of all time.
