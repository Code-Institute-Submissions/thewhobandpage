About Me

What style do we play?

We have played rock music with many cult-favorites known today.

How long have we made music?

The original band started playing together back in 1964. Two of the original four members have died over the years.

Why hire me?

We have toured now for 54 years off and on still with a ton of energy. Also, there are only three bands who have more experience, The Rolling Stones, The Beach boys, and Golden Earring.
